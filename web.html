About Us

"I Purple BTS" is a platform celebrating BTS's legacy, offering official merchandise, limited-edition albums, and exclusive products for ARMY. Inspired by V's iconic "I purple you," symbolizing trust and love, this site connects fans to BTS through handpicked collections and rare memorabilia.
